The Taj Mahal

LOCATION

The Taj Mahal is located in Agra, Uttar Pradesh, India. It covers an area of about 17 hectares.

Why was it made?

It was commissioned in 1632 by the Mughal emperor Shah Jahan (reigned from 1628 to 1658) to house the tomb of his favourite wife, Mumtaz Mahal; it also houses the tomb of Shah Jahan himself.

Interesting Facts

  • The Taj Mahal is made up of white marble.
  • The name Taj Mahal means “crown of palaces”.
  • The construction of the Taj Mahal took around 20 years.
